Accommodations

Wakinyantuwan Tiwahe offers rustic accommodations which will provide you with a peaceful experience. You may chose to sleep in one of our cabins or in a tipi (tepee) on the prairie beneath the stars. You will discover the joy of escaping from modern life as you enjoy a restful visit.

Our cabins are charming and provide bare essentials for your visit. You can wander the area or relax in the hammock by the creek. There is no electricity, television or phones. You will be "entertained" daily with the natural and beautiful scenery, paint horses as they graze, and the beautiful sky each day.
